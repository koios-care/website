Design system

The Koios palette.

Built on a single brand maroon and warm paper neutrals, with one explorable secondary accent and a tight set of semantic signals. Colour is used sparingly — if it appears, it’s saying something.

The locked KOIOS colour, #5B112F, and its tonal range. Used for headlines accents, primary actions, the logo and dark sections.
